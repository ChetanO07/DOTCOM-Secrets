About DotCom Secrets & The Value Ladder

In Section Four of DotCom Secrets, Russell Brunson reveals the seven core sales funnel types that power successful online businesses. These aren't just random funnels—each one serves a specific purpose on the Value Ladder.

The Value Ladder is a strategic approach where you offer increasing levels of value at increasing price points. Customers start at the bottom (low commitment, high value) and naturally ascend to higher-ticket offers as they experience results and build trust with you.

Your mission: Master all 7 funnels to create a complete business ecosystem that attracts leads, converts buyers, builds recurring revenue, and maximizes customer lifetime value.
